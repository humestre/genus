40 Hz GENUS

Gamma-frequency sensory stimulation — light flicker + audio click train at 40 Hz.

Read before use. Do not use if you have epilepsy, a seizure disorder, or are photosensitive. Stop immediately if you feel dizzy, nauseated, or unwell. This is an experimental, non-medical tool based on published research (Iaccarino et al., 2016; Martorell et al., 2019). It is not a treatment and has not been evaluated by any regulator. Use at your own discretion.

Session

Calibrate to a comfortable level (the protocol targets ~60 dB SPL). Browsers can't read absolute SPL — use a phone SPL meter app held at your ear position to set the level before starting. On iPhone, make sure the side switch is set to ring (no orange showing), otherwise Safari audio is muted.

On iPhone, tap “Start session” — Safari will request fullscreen and request a screen wake lock so the display stays on. Plug into power for long sessions. For best visual flicker, use an iPhone with a ProMotion (120 Hz) display.
